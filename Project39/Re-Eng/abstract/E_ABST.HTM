ABSTRACT

The most interesting administrative practise in this era is re-engineering

The rapid chances of this period forces business adapt itself with boundless technology inorder to survive in its business cycle and also to develop its organization.

Re-engineering is an answer for those. It is an organization thoery which focus on changing perception of day-to-day practise. It is a new theory confirmed by many business sectors in the United States that it is the only means leading to the end side with the changes.
