บทคัดย่อ

Abstract
              Netscape Communication has introduced the concept of  plug - ins to Navigator 2.0.Basically, a plug - in is a special type of  program that extends the capabilities of  Netscape Navigator or other browser . For example, you can add plug - ins that enable you to visit virtual reality Web sites to listen to live radio broadcasts over the Net or to carry on a near - to - live " conversation ." There many plug - ins for browser that you can download various sites throughout the Internet. Once you download a plug - in and install it, the plug - in's capabilities become a part of  Browser.
